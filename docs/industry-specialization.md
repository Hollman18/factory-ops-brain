# Industry specialization

## Purpose
Allow the same architecture to adapt across different manufacturing sectors without redesigning the whole agent.

## Core idea
The template is industry-general, but each deployment may emphasize different modules, KPIs, terminology, and risk patterns.

## Example specialization axes
- food and beverage
- dairy
- plastics
- packaging
- pharma
- automotive
- metals
- textiles
- continuous process industries
- discrete manufacturing

## What may vary by industry
- most critical KPIs
- typical failure patterns
- quality vocabulary
- unit conventions
- hierarchy conventions
- alert severity thresholds
- preferred report style

## Rule
Keep the architecture stable, but allow deployments to enrich terminology, KPI semantics, risk patterns, and reporting emphasis according to the organization’s industrial context.
