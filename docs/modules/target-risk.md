# Target risk module

## Purpose
Assess whether current trends create risk against daily, weekly, or monthly goals.

## Operational meaning
This module should convert current performance into business risk language: whether the operation is on track, drifting, or likely to miss the target if nothing changes.

## Expected analytical behavior
- compare actual performance against targets
- identify trajectory toward target miss or overperformance
- explain the main operational drivers of target risk
- escalate to management when impact is material
- distinguish temporary shortfall from sustained target risk

## Role-aware interpretation
- Manager/director: risk, expected impact, recommendation
- Supervisor: where performance recovery must happen operationally
- Maintenance/Quality: how technical or quality behavior contributes to target risk

## Alerting expectations
This module should drive heartbeat escalation whenever target-miss risk becomes material.

## Missing-data rule
If target, production, or performance data is not connected or not reliable, state that clearly and recommend contacting the data administrator.
