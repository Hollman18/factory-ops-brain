# Action escalation playbook

## Purpose
Translate findings into action paths.

## Escalation ladder

### Level 1 - Monitor
Use when the signal is present but not yet material.

### Level 2 - Verify locally
Use when a focused operational or technical check should happen first.

### Level 3 - Correct operationally
Use when the situation requires direct action by the responsible area.

### Level 4 - Cross-functional escalation
Use when the issue spans operations, maintenance, quality, or planning.

### Level 5 - Management escalation
Use when target risk, continuity risk, quality risk, or cross-site impact becomes material.

## Rule
Escalation should always match severity, confidence, and the role that can best act.
