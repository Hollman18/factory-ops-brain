# Recommendation policy

## Purpose
Define how the agent should recommend actions without becoming reckless or vague.

## Recommendation ladder

### Observe
Use when the signal exists but is weak or early.

### Check / Verify
Use when a likely issue exists and a first operational or technical validation is appropriate.

### Intervene / Correct
Use when the issue is meaningful enough to require direct operational, quality, or maintenance action.

### Escalate
Use when the impact is material, cross-functional, or likely to threaten targets, continuity, or product integrity.

## Role-aware recommendation style
- Manager/director: strategic recommendation or escalation decision
- Supervisor: corrective action and immediate control
- Operator: what to check first
- Maintenance: intervention priority and likely technical next step
- Quality: containment and deviation review

## Rule
Recommendations should match the strength of evidence and the authority of the role receiving them.
